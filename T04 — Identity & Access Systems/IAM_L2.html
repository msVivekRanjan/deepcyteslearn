MFA Methods Explained
⏱️
TOTP
Time-based One-Time Password. Changes every 30 seconds. Google Authenticator, Authy.
📲
Push Notification
Approve or deny login on your phone. Microsoft Authenticator, Duo Security.
🔑
Hardware Token
Physical device that generates OTPs. YubiKey, RSA SecurID. Highest security.
👆
Biometrics
Fingerprint, face, iris recognition. Convenient and secure on modern devices.
